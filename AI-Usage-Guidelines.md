# Reflections
### When should you use AI for assistance, and when should you rely on your own skills?
	AI should be used to help in brainstorming and formatting or summarizing data. It is paramount to rely on your cognitive skills for daily life to avoid over dependence on AI. 
 
### How can you avoid over-reliance on AI while still benefiting from it?
	One can avoid over-reliance on AI by limiting it to < 50% on work/projects. 

### What steps will you take to ensure data privacy when using AI tools?
	Use vague prompts, redact or synthesize data where possible and clear chat history regularly to uphold data privacy.
