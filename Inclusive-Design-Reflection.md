# Reflection
### How can you adjust your communication style to be more inclusive of neurodivergent users and teammates?
	Communication with neurodivergent users and teammates involves being clear and direct while offering flexibility in communication channels as some individuals prefer written over verbal. Therefore, I will be direct and offer multiple communication channels. 

### What are some common UX or communication pitfalls that might make Focus Bear less accessible or supportive?
	Some of the common UX or communication pitfalls include strict blocking modes and initial setup confusion.


### What is one practical change you can make in your work to better support vulnerable populations?
	One change I look to employ in my daily work is utilising plain English in all written documents to reduce cognitive overload and the need to disclose a disability. This in turn will improve trust.
