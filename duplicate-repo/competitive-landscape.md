# Reflection
### What makes Focus Bear different from these apps?
These apps all differ from Focus Bear in various ways, namely,
Freedom and Cold Turkey only focus on blocking distractions while Tiimo is a mood tracking visual planner with no enforcement and Routinery solely focuses on routine execution. Focus Bear is the only one that combines routine guidance, cross-device distraction blocking and a neurodivergent-first design in one product. 

### If you were a user, why would you choose Focus Bear over competitors?
I would choose Focus Bear over competitors as it is the only application that enforces distraction blocking while walking you through the routine in realtime. 

### What’s one feature that other apps have that Focus Bear doesn’t?
Other apps such as Tiimo have a mood tracking and wellness planner, this helps the user document how they are feeling while completing their daily tasks. 

### Based on your research, what’s one improvement you think Focus Bear could make?
I would suggest enforcing smarter, adaptive scheduling that allows a user to reschedule something in the routine to a different time during the day.
