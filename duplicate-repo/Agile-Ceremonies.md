# Research & Learn
### What are the main Agile ceremonies, and what is their purpose?
Agile ceremonies are daily meetings that facilitate planning, collaboration and improvement within teams. 
* Daily Stand-ups - These are short meetings for team members to share their progress and discuss any obstacles faced.
* Sprint Planning (Scrum) vs. Continuous Prioritization (Kanban) - This is where the What, How and When for the product is defined. The team sets a goal and works on backlog items
* Retrospectives - this is where the team reflects on the previous sprint and identifies any improvements that need to be made.
* Backlog Refinement- This is a session to review, update and prioritize any items in the product to ensure they are ready for future sprints

### How do Agile teams collaborate asynchronously and across time zones?
Agile teams collaborate asynchronously across time zones by having their daily standups online, prioritizing documentation by utilizing cloud-based project management tools like fireflies and embracing ‘default-to-async’ communication via Discord to ensure progression and avoid bottlenecks.
