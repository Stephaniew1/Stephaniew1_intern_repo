# Reflection
### Have you ever experienced or witnessed great teamwork and professionalism? What made it effective?
	I believe great teamwork and professionalism starts with direct and respectful communication. The greatest teams I have witnessed applied this throughout the entire duration of the project. This in turn greatly improved the quality of work that they produced both individually and as a team. 

### What steps can you take to make sure your communication is clear, respectful, and inclusive?
	I would implement several things, namely, multiple communication channels to ensure everyone is accommodated, practice active listening, be clear and concise and send agendas ahead of time. 

### How can you help create a positive, fun, and professional work environment?
	I can help create a positive work environment by practicing positive reinforcement, maintain open and transparent communication and encouraging collaboration. 

### How can you prepare effectively for meetings with supervisors and colleagues?
	I would read the necessary agendas before the meeting, review previous notes, prepare a progress update and strategic questions. 

### What would it look like to be proactive during your internship?
	Proactivity during my internship includes, attending all stand up meetings, showing initiative on tasks, seeking regular feedback, documenting everything and being visible. 

### How might you follow up with colleagues or supervisors if you need information/action from them? How would you escalate it further if you don't get a response and when would you escalate?
	I would follow up after 2 days and switch communication channels if they are still unreachable. Escalation would only occur after 1 unanswered follow-up by cc’ing their manager (or mine).

### What are some things you won't do?
	I will not ignore feedback, use company time for personal business, miss deadlines, or engage in workplace politics and drama.
